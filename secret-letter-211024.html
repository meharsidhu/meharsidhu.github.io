Secret Letter 2021-10-24

Gugu, this is for the times when you say, "tu chhad jana menu"

I let you in
in places I didn't know I kept secret
so tell me,
how can we become strangers again
when you know parts of me
that I didn't

This is for the times when you say, "mea tenu vadhiya lgno hatju"

In all the world,
there is no heart for me
like yours. I have been
with you everytime, I will be there for you
all the time.

This is for the times when you say, "tu taan chal jana"

don't be afraid
because I'm here for you... forever
I'll love you more that yesterday,
more than today and keep
on going until forever.

And for re-assurance

you are the finest, loveliest, tenderest, and
most beautiful person I have ever known -
and even that is an understatement
